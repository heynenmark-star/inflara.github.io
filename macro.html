Why This Matters

Traditional monetary systems can expand money supply over time while consumer prices respond with delays and distortions.

Inflara is designed as a rule-based response to that macroeconomic problem.

Key Measures

M1

The narrowest measure of money, usually including physical currency and the most liquid deposits.

M2

A broader monetary aggregate that includes M1 plus additional savings-type balances and near-money instruments.

M3

A broader measure again. In the euro area it is still published, while official US M3 is no longer maintained.

Inflara is an open, rule-based monetary protocol designed to be auditable and constrained.
